WHO AM I?

Samantha is currently a junior at the University of Miami hoping to make it big in the fashion industry. She is majoring in print and broadcast journalism and minoring in marketing. Samantha’s hometown is sunny Boca Raton, Florida. She has a twin sister Sydney and another younger sister Spencer.

While in high school she knew that she wanted to pursue a career in fashion and took as many opportunities she could that would allow her to gain the knowledge and skills necessary to make it in the industry. Samantha worked for a clothing boutique for four years pulling looks for clients and getting a better understanding of what works for different types of women. During the summer leading into my junior year of high school, she had the amazing opportunity to work for designer Mara Hoffman in New York City. As she entered her college career she continued to take as many opportunities as she could including interning at Teen Vogue Magazine, Rachel Zoe’s Design Team, and Ocean Drive Magazine.

When she is not concentrating on school and internship opportunities, Samantha enjoys working out and participating in various Mud races such as Spartan or Tough Mudder. An avid believer in health and having a healthy lifestyle she always makes time in her busy schedule to workout. She also enjoys cooking. When she was younger she wanted to become a chef and thought that she would end up going to culinary school. Samantha possesses the dedication, determination, energy and personality to be successful in the fashion industry. A highly organized, proactive personality is what is needed and she is confident that she possesses these attributes.
